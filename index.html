About Gem Collector

Welcome to Gem Collector! Dive into a vast open world and put your survival skills to the test.

Your goal is to collect as many glowing gems as you can to rack up your score. But beware! The world is teeming with relentless ants, swooping bats, and lumbering bears. Each encounter will cost you health.

Survive as long as you can, chase the high score, and fight for your spot on the leaderboards!
